Grilled Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Well today I have something that will change your peanut butter loving life. If you haven’t tried it before, I’d like to introduce you to this Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe. After one bite of this ooey gooey peanut buttery sandwich, you will never, ever be able to eat another “regular” PB&J again.

Instructions

  1. Preheat a griddle to 350 degrees F, or preheat a nonstick pan over medium heat until warm.
  2. While griddle/pan is preheating, make sandwiches:
  3. Take each piece of bread and spread 1 TBS of peanut butter on the sides that will be the middle of the sandwich (the sides that will touch).
  4. Spread 1 TBS of jelly evenly over the peanut butter on two of the pieces of bread.
  5. Put the pieces of bread together to make two sandwiches.
  6. Spread each piece of bread with ½ TBS of butter.
  7. Place sandwich on your preheated griddle or nonstick pan.
  8. Cook for 2-4 minutes or until the sandwich is golden brown.
  9. Flip and cook for another 2-4 minutes until golden brown.
  10. Serve immediately for the best ooey, gooey, melty peanut butter and jelly experience of your life!
